GEOMETRY HARMONY

Our TRIME tents are characterised by an original design which offers a wide array of possibilities for interior decor. Thanks to our self-supporting roof the space is not cluttered by pillars and you can additionally remove walls, regardless of the size of the tent

Already at the design stage, the installation of various types of equipment and lighting was taken into account. As a result, the well thought-out arrangement of load-bearing elements and fixings gives you the unique possibility to adapt the interior to your own personal style.

Perfect for weddings and banquets. High, transparent walls give a sense of space, and gentle lighting puts guests in a magical mood. The elegant and original structure of the construction attracts notice and admiration even from a distance. TRIME tents work faultlessly and safely in any weather conditions.

The innovative design of the TRIME party tent has been registered in the Patent Office of the Republic of Poland.

Tansegrity solutions have made their way into a number of structural designs, such as construction of bridges. We are at the forefront of technology, being the first ones to apply it in the construction of portable buildings – our TRIME tents.

The main idea behind Tensegrity is to assign a specific role to each element of the structure. Every detail is optimized to perform its task to its best. As a result, we have produced a light and extremely robust structure with optimal force distribution.
